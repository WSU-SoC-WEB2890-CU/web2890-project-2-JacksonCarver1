About Us


CREST Consulting is comprised of a group of valuation professionals providing a full array of residential and commercial real estate valuation and consulting services. Our team of experienced staff appraisers are committed to providing attentive valuation services focusing on accurate analysis and consistent report delivery time.

Service Commitment:

Quality Control

Two Tier Review Process with State Certified Appraisers to ensure high quality reports.

Preparation Ensured

All appraisals are prepared in compliance with the requirements of the Uniform Standards of Professional Appraisal Practice (USPAP), adopted by the Appraisal Standards Board (ASB) of The Appraisal Foundation.

Skilled Staff

Highly trained and qualified professional STAFF appraisers ready to help any customer in need!

Extensive Internal Database

Electronic Order Management, enabling quick and reliable status updates to our customers.
